How To Play

    1. A riddle will be displayed on the screen and you have to make a guess.

    2. If you correctly guess the riddle's answer, you win.

    3. You have 3 attempts to guess the answer.

    4. If you run out of attempts, the game ends and you lose.

    5. Additionally, you'll see a "hint" button above the riddle.

    6. If you press the button, a visual clue to the answer will be displayed.
